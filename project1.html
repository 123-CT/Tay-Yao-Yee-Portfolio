'FitEase' The Workout Guide App

FitEase serves as a comprehensive guide to various workout types, providing users with essential information such as price, calories burned, required equipment, suitable user groups, benefits, and safety precautions. Designed for clarity and convenience, the app helps users choose exercises that match their goals, fitness level, and lifestyle, making it easier to build informed and effective fitness routines.

IDE: Android Studio

Programming Language: Kotlin
