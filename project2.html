ART Mobile Prototype

This project is a high fidelity Automated Rapid Transport (ART) mobile prototype, focusing on an interactive map feature. Users can easily explore the city and filter points of interest, such as restaurants, tourist attractions, schools, and more directly on the map. The prototype emphasizes user-friendly navigation, clear visual hierarchy, intuitive interactions, and accessibility features, allowing users to plan routes efficiently and discover nearby attractions with ease.
